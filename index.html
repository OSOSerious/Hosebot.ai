About Hose-Bot

Hose-Bot is an innovative AI-powered system designed to enhance safety and efficiency in the construction industry. By utilizing advanced computer vision and 3D imaging, Hose-Bot eliminates risks associated with traditional concrete placement methods, ensuring safer working environments and increased productivity.

Key Features

Specialized Hose System

Delivers concrete precisely through hoses directly into prepared molds, ensuring accurate placement.

Green Cubic Molds

Unique molds designed for shaping concrete into desired structural forms with high precision.

Controlled Pumping Mechanism

Enables consistent and regulated concrete flow, enhancing overall pouring efficiency.

Advanced AI Integration

Utilizes AI and 3D computer vision for optimal concrete placement and operational control.

Modular Construction

Supports modular construction techniques, allowing for scalable and adaptable building processes.

Environmental Efficiency

Reduces concrete waste through precise application, promoting sustainable construction practices.

Operational Process

The operational workflow of Hose-Bot ensures systematic and efficient concrete pouring:

1. Mold Arrangement

Green cubic molds are strategically arranged in the desired structural pattern.

2. Hose Positioning

Hoses are precisely positioned to align with each mold, preparing for concrete delivery.

3. Controlled Pumping

Concrete is pumped through the hoses, filling each mold systematically and uniformly.

4. Quality Assurance

AI-driven systems monitor the pouring process, ensuring consistency and quality.
